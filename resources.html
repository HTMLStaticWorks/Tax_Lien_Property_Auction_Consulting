Glossary

Investor Glossary of Terms

Filter terms below by clicking on alphabetical characters to learn municipal real estate definitions.

Bid-Down Interest

auction bidding method where competitors start at maximum interest rates and bid down. The lowest interest rate wins the certificate.

Certificate of Sale

Legal document issued by the county upon receiving bidding payment, certifying the investor has priority lien claim against the parcel.

Foreclosure

Legal court process initiated by the certificate holder to claim title property ownership when taxes remain unredeemed.

Quiet Title action

a separate lawsuit filed in circuit court to wipe away prior owner claims and deliver clear title guarantee on acquired auction deeds.

Redemption Period

Statutory timeframe (typically 1-3 years) allowing the property owner to pay the county back taxes, principal, and accrued interest.

Tax Lien

a statutory claim placed against real property by local governments for delinquent property tax payments, holding top priority status.
