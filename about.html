Our Philosophy

Rules We Invest By

Unlike speculative bidding agencies, we adhere to strict standards to protect investor capital.

1. Data Over Rumor

We perform title searches, GIS mapping audits, and code inspection reviews on every parcel. We do not participate in counties with incomplete public records.

2. Principal Preservation First

High yields are secondary to capital security. We avoid hazardous zoning areas, industrial EPA properties, and bankruptcy filings that lock capital.

3. Bid System Discipline

We enforce hard premium caps and walk away from over-competitive bidding structures where premium payouts suppress net interest yields.
