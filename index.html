Educational Core

Understanding Tax Liens & Auction Procedures

When property owners fall delinquent on their property taxes, county municipalities issue tax lien certificates to recoup funding. These certificates are sold via public auction, where investors bid down the interest rate or bid up a premium.

If the homeowner redeems the taxes, the investor receives their principal investment plus the high statutory interest rate. If they fail to redeem within the state's window (typically 1 to 3 years), the investor can initiate foreclosure and acquire the property free and clear of mortgages.
