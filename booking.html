Document Preparation Guidance

For corporate, trust, or high-volume strategy calls, preparing these documents beforehand accelerates setup:

Completed IRS W-9

Counties require valid Taxpayer Identification numbers (TIN) to process your interest distributions on redemption.

Entity Organization Document

If bidding via a corporate LLC or holding trust, have your Good Standing certificates ready for county portal mapping.

Bank Wire/Funds Verification

Bidding deposits require cleared bank wires. Prepare wire coordinates matching your registered bidder profile.
