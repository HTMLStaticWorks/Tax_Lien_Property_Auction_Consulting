How to Prepare for Your Advisory Call

Help us maximize your session value by gathering these details beforehand:

1

Define Your Maximum Capital Allocation

Counties require up-front cash deposits (usually 10% of planned bids) before the auction opens.

2

Select Your Target State Jurisdictions

Decide if you prefer Arizona/Florida certificate states (passive yields) or Texas/California deed states (direct title).

3

Isolate Specific Parcels for Title Audits

If you have active county parcel IDs, bring them to the session for a quick-run GIS appraiser test.
